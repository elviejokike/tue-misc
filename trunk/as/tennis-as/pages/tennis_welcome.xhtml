Why Tennis?

Tennis is fun and exciting when you have a favorite player to route for. It keeps one motivated to watch the matches and we believe it is a fun sport. We believe people play tennis for the love of the sport. It sure would keep a person in shape and it is one of those sports where you are on your own, whether you win or lose you are mostly responsible for the outcome. Of course you have many supporters and fans and family on your side but only you can get the trophy and check when you win a tournament.

But, behind Tennis - the game - there is a lot of history: evolution, players, classical games, tournaments, statistics which is valuable to know when you are getting into it as a serious supporter.
